Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Book the accommodation.

    You can use the website like Booking.com to reserve an accommodation.

  2. Download the offline google map.

    It is important when you don't have internet to find the way.

  3. Bring some medicine

    If you get cold, you can take medicine rather than go to hospital.

  4. Keep the emergency contact with you.

    Others can reach out to your parents when you need helps.
